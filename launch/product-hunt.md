## Description

Every time you're interrupted while coding, you lose 23 minutes of deep focus (Gloria Mark, UC Irvine). Your editor saves your files — but not what you were *thinking*.

Dev Checkpoint fixes that.

**One keystroke saves:**
• Git branch + what you changed
• Active file, cursor position, symbol you were working on
• Open TODOs and FIXMEs
• Recently touched files
• Terminal history

**3 seconds to restore** — a human-readable narrative: *"You were fixing JWT refresh token rotation in auth/middleware.ts, line 47."*

**Why it's different:**
→ Zero cloud, zero AI APIs, zero subscriptions
→ Everything stored locally on your machine
→ Works immediately after install, zero config
→ Open source MIT, 62 tests

Free forever. No catch.
